Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to experience a VR tour?

You’ll need a VR headset and a stable internet connection. We recommend devices like Oculus Quest or similar.

How long are the tours?

Most tours last between 30 minutes to 1 hour, depending on the destination.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 24 hours before the scheduled tour.
